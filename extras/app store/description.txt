Fish Hoek Weather


SHORT DESCRIPTION:
------------------

LIVE weather updates from Fish Hoek beach


LONG DESCRIPTION:
-----------------

Get LIVE weather updates direct from Fish Hoek beach.

Swipe left & right to view Wind, Temperature & Barometer readings.

Set app to refresh automatically in the settings menu. This way you will always have up to date readings when you open the app.


This is a *beta* version with a limited set of features currently implemented.
